1st International Conference & on Advances in Computing & Communications(ICACC-2022)
22-23 June, 2022

(ADGITM Conference ID: 55379)

The purpose of International Conference on Advances in Computing and Communications is to bring together researchers, scholars and developers under one roof to exchange information and share experiences and research results for an overall growth of science & technology.

This conference will provide an excellent international forum to researchers and practitioners from academia as well as industry to discuss their latest research results, ideas, developments and applications in all areas of engineering technology and innovation. It also intends to provide a conducive environment to students so that they can augment their knowledge in different areas of research

Once you accomplish the presentation in the Conference, after which papers will be submitted to IEEE for consideration for possible publication in IEEE Xplore. Final decision lies with the Technical Program Integrity Committee of IEEE whether the papers are fit for publication in IEEE Xplore or not. Accordingly payment of registration fee doesn't ensure publication of paper in IEEE Xplore.

About Us

Dr. Akhilesh Das Gupta Institute of Technology and Management, formerly known as Northern India Engineering College (NIEC), New Delhi was established by BBDES, LUCKNOW in the year 2003. ADGITM has an aesthetically designed campus spread over approximately 9 acres of lush green environment at Shastri Park.
